******Project Overview

This project involves a detailed analysis of Amazon product categories. The goal was to clean, normalize, and analyze the dataset to uncover insights about product distribution, ratings, reviews, discounts, price ranges, and revenue potential across different categories.


***What I Did

Cleaned and normalized the dataset using  SQL to ensure data quality and consistency.

Performed category-wise analysis to identify dominant and niche product categories.

Analyzed average ratings and reviews to understand customer satisfaction and engagement.

Examined discount patterns to infer promotional strategies across categories.

Investigated price ranges and revenue potential to highlight market focus and opportunities.

Created clear, simple, and professional reports summarizing key insights.


***Tools and Skills Used

Excel for initial data cleaning and exploration.

SQL for advanced data cleaning, normalization, and analysis.

Power BI for dashboard creation and data visualization.


***Key Insights

Electronics is the strongest category with the most products, good ratings, highest discounts, and top revenue potential.

Home & Kitchen products are the most engaging with the highest reviews and solid ratings.

Office Products have the highest customer satisfaction despite smaller size.

Toys & Games are niche with less promotion but stable demand.

The low-price range dominates, showing Amazon's focus on affordability.
