Amazon Data Insights


Category Distribution

Ratings

Office Products have the highest average rating which shows customers are very satisfied with them.

Reviews

Home & Kitchen products get the most reviews which means they are very popular and widely used.

Discounts

Electronics offer the biggest discounts which suggests sellers use promotions to attract buyers in this category.

Toys & Games have the lowest discounts meaning they rely less on promotions.


Price Range

The low-price range has the highest number of products showing that affordability is a big focus for Amazon’s marketplace.

Revenue Potential

Electronics lead again with the highest average discounted price making them the top category for revenue potential.
